Estate Essentials


Estate Essentials is a web-based platform that connects landlords, tenants, buyers, and sellers of real estate properties. By providing a centralized platform for renting, buying, and selling real estate, a rented and buying selling portal can streamline the process, increase the efficiency of transactions, and make it easier for users to find the information they need. Taking house/office /shop on rent is always a better way than purchasing it, if a person does not have sufficient amount of money. This web portal will give all the details of landlords along with their rented property details in different areas of a city. Any needy person can search these accommodations and further communicate with that landowner. Additionally, the use of advanced technologies such as machine learning can help to personalize the experience for users and provide more accurate property.


In a manual system, communication between buyers, sellers, landlords, and tenants can be slow and time-consuming. This can lead to delays in transactions and lost opportunities. In a manual system, processes such as property search, negotiations, and payments can be time-consuming and may require multiple steps and interactions. This can lead to delays and increased costs. It can be difficult to verify the authenticity of information about properties, transactions, and agreements. This can lead to confusion and mistrust among parties involved. Information about properties can become outdated quickly and may not be accurate. This can lead to frustration for buyers, sellers, and renters who are relying on the information to make informed decisions. Working people who don’t belong to the city, where they have to do job, they always in search of safe house where they can live safely, happily with their loved ones. Working person always try to find out a house near by their office location. Manually these things are very tiresome and frustrating. Similarly, a house owner always wants a reliable tenant It is also very difficult for the owner to search reliable tenant manually. He/she needs to put a lot off efforts for doing this and for this task they have to pay additionally to property dealers or brokers. A person willing to sell his/her house is also not able to find out suitable customer easily. A manual system may not have the same level of reach and exposure as a web-based platform To resolve these types of problems this portal has been developed. This portal will contain all the reliable data of house/shop/office premises. Any use can search these accommodations. This portal will also show the details for buying and selling of constructed property. Agreement details, rules for buying and purchasing according to the norms all things will be displayed on the portal. Sellers, Landlord, buyers all have to get registered on the portal for any kind of communication and action. Vatsu consultant can also get registered to the portal to show the vastu related tips on the portal as well as to provide consultation.ML model will predict the cost of the house which a seller wants to sell or a buyer wants to purchase.

Objective:


• To put Buyer, sellers, landlords in one roof so that they can be searched and viewed by the needy one on a single click.
• Providing a platform for the owners to showcase their constructed property details for renting/selling
• To spread awareness related to renting and selling constructed property so that nobody can misguide any one.
• Streamline all types of property on this portal.
• Ease of user for searching accommodation without wondering here and there.
• No broker requirement.
• Vastu consultation.
